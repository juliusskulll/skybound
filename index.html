Getting Started

Skybound is a modified skyblock SMP where all players begin on an above-average sized starting island. The server is not just about normal survival. It is built around limited resources, daily choices, social experiments, minigames, trust, betrayal, and the mystery of the portal.

At the start, keep inventory will be enabled for at least 1 or 2 days. This gives players time to get settled, build a base, gather early resources, and prepare before the server becomes more dangerous.

The main goal is to survive, expand the island, trade with other players, complete tasks, take part in events, and use the portal to collect rare resources from other worlds.
